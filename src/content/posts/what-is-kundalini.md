Jane's article frames Kundalini as more than a physical yoga style. The source
article connects it with creative energy, the cakra system, and the gradual
loosening of patterns that can keep a person stuck at survival level.

The beta version should probably keep three ideas prominent:

1. Kundalini practice is treated gently and reflectively, not as a forceful
   mechanical technique.
2. The cakras provide a map for working with levels of consciousness and human
   experience.
3. The teaching tone is careful: inner work should respect readiness, safety,
   and the whole person.

This summary intentionally links to the original rather than copying the full
article body while the public beta is being reviewed.
